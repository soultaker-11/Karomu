Asset Recovery

We perform lawful and professional asset recovery services to assist in reclaiming collateral.

Process

  1. Report & Intake — submit details and documents.
  2. Investigation & Tracking — we investigate and locate assets.
  3. Recovery Operation — lawful recovery actions executed.
  4. Return & Documentation — assets returned with paperwork.

Typical timelines: investigation 3–7 days, recovery 7–30 days depending on case complexity.

Start Intake

Required documents

  • Proof of ownership (vehicle registration, purchase receipt)
  • Valid ID
  • Police report (if applicable)

Fees

  • Assessment fee: 100 (currency)
  • Investigation rate: 50 per hour
  • Success fee: 10% of recovered asset value
